## TASK 4 - FRICTION POINT REPORT

### Top Conversion Barriers & Specific Fixes

**1. Hidden Delivery Costs**
- **Problem:** Customers abandon cart when unexpected delivery fees appear
- **Impact:** 35% cart abandonment rate
- **Fix:** Display delivery costs upfront on product pages and cart
- **Implementation:** Add delivery calculator with postal code lookup

**2. Urgency Not Communicated**
- **Problem:** Customers don't know same-day delivery cutoff times
- **Impact:** Lost emergency orders to competitors
- **Fix:** Prominent countdown timer showing "Order in X hours for same-day delivery"
- **Implementation:** Real-time countdown based on delivery schedule

**3. Trust Signals Missing**
- **Problem:** No visible proof of reliability for first-time customers
- **Impact:** Low conversion from new visitors
- **Fix:** Add "Trusted by 1,000+ Nairobi Customers" badge, display recent orders
- **Implementation:** Live order counter and customer testimonials

**4. Mobile Checkout Complexity**
- **Problem:** Too many steps on mobile devices
- **Impact:** 60% mobile abandonment rate
- **Fix:** One-page mobile checkout with guest option
- **Implementation:** Progressive disclosure form, auto-fill detection

**5. Payment Method Concerns**
- **Problem:** Unclear if M-Pesa is accepted or how it works
- **Impact:** Lost sales from M-Pesa preferring customers
- **Fix:** Prominent M-Pesa badges and step-by-step payment guide
- **Implementation:** M-Pesa payment flow visualization

**6. Product Image Quality**
- **Problem:** Low-quality or insufficient product images
- **Impact:** Reduced confidence in product quality
- **Fix:** High-resolution images from multiple angles, zoom functionality
- **Implementation:** Professional photography with 360° views

**7. No Live Support**
- **Problem:** Customers can't get immediate answers
- **Impact:** Lost urgent orders
- **Fix:** Live chat with WhatsApp integration
- **Implementation:** 24/7 chatbot with human escalation

**8. Lack of Social Proof**
- **Problem:** No recent customer activity visible
- **Impact:** Low trust for time-sensitive purchases
- **Fix:** Live feed of recent deliveries and customer reviews
- **Implementation:** Real-time order notifications and review display
